Goal:

Create a Powerpoint to instruct a student on how to solve a Normal Distribution problems.

Instructions:

For each group of no more than 3, Create a 2 part Normal Distribution Problem.
Create a method where it is incorrect to solve it, give a reason WHY.
Give the correct solution.
With the given answer, give a reason to check the answer to ensure it makes sense in the context of the question.
Do this for each part.
It is optional, but there is a tool to graphically represent the normal distribution problem. You are encouraged to use it.

THE QUESTION:
The first part should be a Normal Distribution where we find probabilities (make it somewhat difficult).
The second part should be an Inverse Normal Distribution problem where we're given probabilities and finding X

EXTENSION:
Include a third part which involves probability trees or finding the mean or standard deviation.


Role Writeup Ideas:
Misconception IDer: Identifies how a student may make a mistake.
Solver: Finds the correct solution.
Checker: Think of ways to ensure the answer makes sense. Prepare the Normal Distribution Diagrams.
